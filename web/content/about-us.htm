Nanogen HealthCare Pvt. Ltd. is a fast growing dynamic company in sales and distribution of high quality lab products.

The launch of NanoGen HealthCare in the year 2009 is based on the market survey of present and future needs of products related to nanotechnology, biotechnology, immunology & pharmaceutical sciences and in vitro diagnostics (IVD).

Our mission is to create an enterprise that is keenly focussed on it's customers and strives to expand it's strength and potential in view of the future of the market.

NanoGens' Corporate office is located at New Delhi and it's distribution network covers Bangalore, Bhopal, Chennai, Hyderabad, Jammu, Kolkata, Lucknow, Pune and Mumbai. Our team of professionals include scientists and sales and marketing persons who are well experienced in the related areas for providing support to the customers. We have a centralized system to monitor the process of procurement to the final delivery of products.
